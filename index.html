What is SimCCS?

SimCCS is an optimization model for integrated system design that enables researchers, stakeholders, and policy makers to design CCS infrastructure networks.

What does SimCCS do?

SimCCS designs CCS networks by leveraging mathematical optimization tools to decide which sources and sinks to open, and where to build CO2 distribution pipelines subject to the goals and constraints of the user.
